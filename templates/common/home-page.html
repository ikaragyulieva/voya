How Voya Works?

Create Your Company Profile

Start your journey by setting up a company profile. This ensures all your clients’ trips are accurately linked to your organization.

Navigate to the Company section, enter your company's details—including the Tax ID—and let us take care of the rest.

{# #}

Create a Client Account

After your company profile is registered, you can create individual client profiles linked to your organization.

Use the New Client section to set up these accounts and ensure all necessary details are captured for seamless trip planning.

{# #}

Submit a Trip Request

Once your company profile is registered, you can create detailed trip requests.

Share specifics like: travel dates and duration, accommodation preferences, desired activities and destinations, budget constraints and any special requirements. The more details you provide, the better we can craft a package perfectly tailored to your clients’ needs.

{# #}

Generate Proposals

Once we receive your request, our agents will begin crafting a professional and tailored trip proposal, ensuring delivery within 48 hours.

{# #}

Collaborate and Finalize

Work hand-in-hand with our travel experts to refine every detail of your trip proposal. Together, we’ll ensure your clients enjoy an unforgettable journey.

{# #}
